Italian Newspaper Publishes World's First AI-Generated Edition

Il Foglio, an Italian conservative liberal daily newspaper, has announced the publication of the world's first edition created entirely by artificial intelligence. The AI-generated four-page supplement, called Il Foglio AI, showcases the potential impact of AI technology on journalism. Articles in the first edition addressed various topics, including U.S. President Donald Trump, Russian President Vladimir Putin, and positive economic developments in Italy. This initiative explores AI's practical applications in daily news production and its implications for the future of journalism.

Leaked Photos Reveal Significant Design Changes in Upcoming iPhone 17

Recent leaks suggest that the iPhone 17 will feature notable design changes, particularly in its camera layout and the introduction of a super-thin "iPhone Air" model. Photos indicate different camera configurations and a thinner, lighter model, potentially replacing the current iPhone Pro, measuring just 0.2 inches in width. The Air model may also include an Action Button instead of the traditional Ring/Mute slider. Pricing details remain undisclosed, but the Air is speculated to be the most affordable option.
